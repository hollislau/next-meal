Next Meal

Welcome to Next Meal! We are a non-profit organization that brings together resources to better assist ongoing hunger needs in our communities.

How it works:

Next Meal allows for information to be requested either by SMS text messaging and/or voice call request. A response will be given on where there are local food distribution locations that are giving meals for that day.

To receive a text response:

  1. Send a text message to: 425-276-6480
  2. The text message should be either - breakfast, lunch or dinner
  3. The user will receive a response a short time later

To receive a voice response:

  1. Users can call 206-429-6617
  2. Prompts will guide callers to enter - breakfast, lunch, or dinner
  3. Callers will receive up to two locations for that day

Hunger Facts in Washington:

  • Washington is the 23rd hungriest state in the nation
  • 1 in 7 Washingtonians relies on SNAP (food stamps), the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program, which is severely threatened by budget cuts
  • 1 in 5 WA kids lives in a household that struggles with hunger
  • Half of all people on SNAP are kids
